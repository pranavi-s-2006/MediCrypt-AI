# MediCrypt Guardian AI — Problem Statement

## Problem
1. Medical records are fragmented across hospitals and clinics with no unified patient history
2. Dangerous drug-drug interactions go undetected in multi-doctor prescription scenarios
3. Emergency responders lack instant access to critical patient data (blood group, allergies)
4. Elderly patients in rural India struggle with English-only medical interfaces
5. Doctor access to patient records is ungoverned, creating privacy risks
6. Handwritten prescriptions are error-prone and difficult to digitize

## Target Users
- Patients (especially elderly, rural, Tamil-speaking)
- Doctors (multi-specialty, seeking safe prescribing)
- Hospital Administrators (managing staff, compliance)
- Caregivers (family members managing elderly patients)
- Emergency Responders (paramedics needing instant access)

## Impact
- Prevents medication errors and adverse drug reactions
- Creates a lifelong, portable medical identity
- Enables informed consent-based data sharing
- Supports Tamil-speaking elderly with voice interfaces
- Saves lives in emergencies with instant QR access
